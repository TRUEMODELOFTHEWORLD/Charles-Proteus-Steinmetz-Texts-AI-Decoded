## Core Definition

Electric waves are Steinmetz's lower-frequency electromagnetic waves, including wireless telegraph waves, Hertzian waves, and the wave aspect of alternating-current fields.

## Modern Equivalent

Radio-frequency electromagnetic waves and propagating fields.

## Why It Matters

This concept links Steinmetz's power-system world to radio, transients, lightning, and wave propagation.
