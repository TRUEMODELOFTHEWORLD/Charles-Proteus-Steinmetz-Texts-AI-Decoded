# Transient Phenomena

Status: seeded from `elementary-lectures-electric-discharges-waves-impulses` and `theory-calculation-transient-electric-phenomena-oscillations`.

## Core Definition

Transient phenomena are temporary electrical conditions that appear when a circuit changes, when energy shifts between magnetic and electrostatic storage, or when distributed systems reveal propagation behavior.
