# Illumination

Status: seeded.

## Core Definition

Illumination is the application of radiation and light to human seeing, measurement, distribution, comfort, and engineering design.

## Steinmetz Context

The later lectures of `Radiation, Light and Illumination` treat illumination as both a physical and physiological problem. This is one of the book's strongest conceptual lessons: lighting cannot be reduced to radiant power alone because visibility depends on the eye and the use-context.

## Modern Equivalent

Illuminating engineering, lighting design, photometry, luminous flux, illuminance, luminance, glare control, and visual ergonomics.

## Why It Matters

It shows Steinmetz working as an engineer of perception as well as an engineer of power.
