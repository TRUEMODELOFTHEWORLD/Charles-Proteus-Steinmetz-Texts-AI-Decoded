# Hysteresis

Status: seeded from alternating-current and theoretical electrical engineering sources.

## Core Definition

Hysteresis is lag or history-dependence in a material's magnetic response, with associated energy loss during cyclic magnetization.

## Modern Equivalent

B-H curve hysteresis, magnetic core loss, transformer and motor material loss, nonlinear magnetic memory.
