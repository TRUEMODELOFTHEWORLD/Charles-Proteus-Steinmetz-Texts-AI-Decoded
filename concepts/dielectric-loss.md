# Dielectric Loss

Dielectric loss is the real-power loss associated with dielectric media under electric stress.

Source-located candidate anchor:

- `Theory and Calculation of Alternating Current Phenomena`, Chapter XIV, `Dielectric Losses`.

Steinmetz treats dielectric behavior with capacity, conductance, susceptance, leakage, dielectric hysteresis, corona, and effective admittance language. This makes the dielectric a real electrical participant rather than a perfect abstract separator.
