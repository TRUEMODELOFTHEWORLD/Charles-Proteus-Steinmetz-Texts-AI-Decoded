# Research Questions

The project should maintain a living research agenda. A question can remain open indefinitely if the evidence is incomplete.

## Seed Questions

1. Did Steinmetz use field language differently from modern textbooks?
2. Did his mathematical treatment preserve physical meaning that later teaching abstracts away?
3. Are there concepts in his transient analysis that are under-taught today?
4. How does his treatment of hysteresis compare to modern magnetic material theory?
5. Did he explicitly reference ether, and in what technical contexts?
6. How does his symbolic method connect to later phasor and complex-number engineering?
7. Where did Tesla and Steinmetz agree or diverge on resonance, high frequency, electric waves, and power transmission?
8. How should Wheeler-style interpretations be mapped onto Steinmetz without confusing interpretation with historical fact?
