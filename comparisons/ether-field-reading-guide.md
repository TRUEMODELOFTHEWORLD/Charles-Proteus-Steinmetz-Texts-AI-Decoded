# Ether-Field Interpretive Reading Guide

Status: seeded policy page.

## Purpose

This guide defines how the archive handles Ken Wheeler-style, Dollard-style, Tesla-era, and other ether-field interpretive readings.

## Required Separation

Every interpretive section must distinguish:

- What Steinmetz explicitly states.
- What modern physics says.
- What the interpretive reading suggests.
- What remains speculative.

## Useful Interpretive Vocabulary

Use only when helpful and label it interpretation:

- Dielectricity
- Magnetism
- Field pressure
- Field inertia
- Hysteresis as field lag or memory
- Magnetism as spatial divergence
- Dielectricity as compression or centripetal tendency
- Force as field gradient

## Forbidden Shortcut

Do not present an ether-field reading as Steinmetz's own meaning unless the wording is directly sourced.
