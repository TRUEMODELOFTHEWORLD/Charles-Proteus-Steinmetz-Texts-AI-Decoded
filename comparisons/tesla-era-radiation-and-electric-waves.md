## Question

How does Steinmetz's treatment of electric waves in `Radiation, Light and Illumination` relate to Tesla-era electrical science?

## Steinmetz Side

Steinmetz classifies wireless waves, Hertzian waves, alternating-current fields, ultraviolet, visible light, and X-rays on a shared radiation scale. His treatment is mathematical, pedagogical, and engineering-oriented.

## Tesla-Era Context

Tesla-era electrical science placed intense emphasis on high-frequency currents, resonance, wireless transmission, disruptive discharge, and the physical reality of fields. Steinmetz overlaps with this world technically, especially in electric waves, high-frequency effects, and lightning/surge phenomena.
