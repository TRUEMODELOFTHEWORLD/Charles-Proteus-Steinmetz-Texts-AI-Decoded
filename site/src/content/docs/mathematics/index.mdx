## Working Mathematical Strands

<div class="research-passages">
  <section>
    <h3>Radiation Scale</h3>
    <p>The first source uses velocity, frequency, and wavelength to place electric waves, visible light, ultraviolet, and X-rays on one continuous scale. The archive keeps Steinmetz's historical notation visible, then translates the relation into modern SI units.</p>
  </section>
  <section>
    <h3>Symbolic Alternating-Current Method</h3>
    <p>The AC sources move from time-varying quantities into symbolic calculation. The core reading problem is not only how to compute with complex numbers, but why resistance and reactance become a geometry of magnitude, phase, and physical energy exchange.</p>
  </section>
  <section>
    <h3>Transients and Natural Terms</h3>
    <p>The transient books require a separate mathematical layer: forced terms, natural terms, exponential decay, oscillatory discharge, line reflections, and distributed constants. These are marked as candidate strands until scan-verified equations are promoted to individual pages.</p>
  </section>
</div>
