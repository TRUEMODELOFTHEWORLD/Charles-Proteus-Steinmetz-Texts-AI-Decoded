## Research Reading Map

<div class="research-passages">
  <section>
    <h3>Start With Physical Meaning</h3>
    <p>Steinmetz often begins from the physical process before compressing it into mathematics. The site now treats that as a rule: first name the energy process, then translate the notation, then compare the modern abstraction.</p>
  </section>
  <section>
    <h3>Then Follow The Math</h3>
    <p>The first mathematical spine is the frequency-wavelength relation in the radiation lectures. The next spine is symbolic AC calculation: resistance, reactance, impedance, phase angle, and power factor.</p>
  </section>
  <section>
    <h3>Keep Interpretation In Its Lane</h3>
    <p>Ether-field readings are preserved as disciplined interpretive layers, never as automatic historical claims. Every such layer is separated from Steinmetz's explicit statements and from modern engineering language.</p>
  </section>
</div>
