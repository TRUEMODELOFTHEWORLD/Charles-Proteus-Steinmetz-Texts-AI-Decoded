## Why This Source Matters

This source looks different from the major textbooks: it is practical engineering investigation. That makes it valuable for understanding how Steinmetz reasoned from symptoms, measurements, apparatus behavior, and system configuration.

It should eventually become part of a "Steinmetz engineering method" section: how he diagnoses failure, distinguishes causes, and moves between mathematical theory and real power-system operation.
