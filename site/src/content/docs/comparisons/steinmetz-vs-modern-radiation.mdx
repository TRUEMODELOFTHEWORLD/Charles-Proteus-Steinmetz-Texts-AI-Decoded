title: Steinmetz vs Modern Radiation
description: How Steinmetz's radiation language maps to modern electrical engineering.
---

## What Steinmetz Says

Radiation is energy in transit. Heat is produced when radiation is intercepted and absorbed.

## What Modern EE Says

Electromagnetic radiation carries energy through fields. Thermal radiation is radiation associated with temperature and absorption/emission processes.

## Are They Equivalent?

Mostly. Steinmetz's older language is still conceptually sharp because it refuses to collapse propagation and absorption into a single vague word.

## Interpretive Reading

Interpretive only: this passage supports field-process thinking, but it should not be overstated as proof of any later ether model.
