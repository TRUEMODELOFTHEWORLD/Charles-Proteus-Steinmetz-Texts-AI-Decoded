## Required Labels

- Steinmetz explicitly states
- Modern equivalent
- Interpretive reading
- Speculative connection
- Mathematical reconstruction
- Historical note
- Needs verification

## Rule

No ether-field interpretation should be presented as historical fact unless Steinmetz explicitly says it.

## Useful Vocabulary

Dielectricity, magnetism, field pressure, field inertia, field gradient, hysteresis as lag or memory, and spatial/counterspatial language may appear only in clearly labeled interpretive sections.
