# Counter-Electromotive Force

Modern equivalents: back EMF, induced opposing voltage, voltage drop, or impedance voltage depending on context.

Source-located candidate passages:

- `Theory and Calculation of Alternating Current Phenomena`, Chapter IV, `Vector Representation`, cleaned OCR lines 303-336.
- `Theory and Calculation of Alternating Current Phenomena`, Chapter V, `Symbolic Method`, cleaned OCR lines 285-293 and 405-410.

Steinmetz usage:

Steinmetz uses `counter e.m.f.` both for actual generated opposition in machines and for AC vector terms representing resistance, reactance, or impedance as opposing electromotive forces.
