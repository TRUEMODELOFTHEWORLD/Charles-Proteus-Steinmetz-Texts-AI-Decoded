# Radiant Heat

Status: seeded from `radiation-light-and-illumination`; quote needs scan verification.

## Original Term

Radiant heat.

## Steinmetz Usage

In Lecture I, Steinmetz criticizes the term because the energy is radiation while traveling and becomes heat only when absorbed.

## Modern Equivalent

Thermal radiation, when the radiation is emitted due to temperature and carries energy that may be absorbed as heat.

## Conceptual Meaning

The term can mislead readers into thinking heat itself travels as a substance. Steinmetz's distinction is sharper: radiation travels; heat is produced at absorption.

## Status

Still used colloquially, but modern physics usually treats heat as energy transfer, not a substance in transit.
