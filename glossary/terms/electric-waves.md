# Electric Waves

Status: seeded.

## Original Term

Electric waves.

## Modern Equivalent

Radio-frequency electromagnetic waves in many contexts; more broadly, propagating electromagnetic disturbances.

## Steinmetz Usage

In Lecture I, electric waves include wireless telegraph waves, Hertzian waves, and the wave aspect of alternating-current fields.

## Status

Partly renamed. "Electromagnetic waves" is the modern general term.
