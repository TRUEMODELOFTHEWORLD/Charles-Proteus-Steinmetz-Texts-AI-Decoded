# Effective Resistance

Modern equivalents: AC resistance, equivalent resistance, real-power-loss resistance.

Steinmetz usage:

Steinmetz distinguishes true ohmic resistance from effective resistance. Effective resistance represents total real-power expenditure in AC, including loss mechanisms such as magnetic hysteresis, dielectric hysteresis, secondary currents, leakage, and nonuniform current distribution.

```math
P = I^2 r_\mathrm{eff}
```
